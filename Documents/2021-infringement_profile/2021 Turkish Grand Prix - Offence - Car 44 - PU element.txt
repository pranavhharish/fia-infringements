From The Stewards To The Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 9 Date 08 October 2021 Time 12:00 2021 TURKISH GRAND PRIX 7 - 10 October 2021 The StewardsThe Stewards, having received a report from the Technical Delegate (document 7), have considered the following matter and determine the following: No / Driver 44 - Lewis Hamilton Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 11:31 Session Practice 1 Fact The following Power Unit Element has been used : 4th Internal Combustion Unit (ICE) Offence Breach of Article 23.2 a) of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Drop of 10 grid positions. Reason The penalty is imposed in accordance with 23.2 b). Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
