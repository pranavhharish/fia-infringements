From The Stewards To The Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 TeamDocument 24 Date 10 September 2021 Time 20:01 2021 ITALIAN GRAND PRIX 9 - 12 September 2021 The StewardsThe Stewards, having received a report from the Technical Delegate (document 16), have considered the following matter and determine the following: No / Driver 77 - Valtteri Bottas Competitor Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team Time 18:04 Session Qualifying Fact The following Power Unit Elements have been used 4th Internal Combustion Unit (ICE) 4th Turbo Charger (TC) 4th Motor Generator Unit – Heat (MGU-H) 4th Motor Generator Unit – Kinetic (MGU-K) Offence Breach of Article 23.2 a) of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Decision Required to start the race from the rear of the starting grid. Reason The penalty is imposed in accordance with 23.2 b). Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
