2023 M ONACO GRAND PRIX 26 - 28 May 2023 The StewardsFrom The Stewards To The Team Manager, Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 TeamDocument 16 Date 26 May 2023 Time 18:25 The Stewards, having received a report from the Race Director, have considered the following matter and determine the following: No / Driver 63 - George Russell Competitor Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team Time 17:52 Session Practice 2 Fact Pit lane speeding – 60.2 km/h Infringment Breach of Article 34.7 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations and non- compliance with Race Director’s Event Note (item 13, document 13). Decision The competitor (Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team) is fined €100. Reason Car 63 exceeded the pit lane speed limit which is set at 60 km/h for this event by 0.2 km/h. Competitors are reminded that they have the right to appeal certain decisions of the Stewards, in accordance with Article 15 of the FIA International Sporting Code and Chapter 4 of the FIA Judicial and Disciplinary Rules, within the applicable time limits.
